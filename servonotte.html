Servonotte

Servonotte is part of the Castiglioni Servi family. The clothes stand is designed with a polypropylene base and steel frame, available in the following colours: black, aluminium or white.




3D modelling

I drew the profile of the mast and base with Bezier curves, thus I used the function Rotational Surface to get the entire structure. Very hard was draw the crutches. To do that I made, with Hermite curves, several curved tube placed at suitable. The complex geometry is then obtained by composing the tubes.
